# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 25, 2026

## 1. Zero-PII & Data Sanitization
Rta is designed to minimize collection of Personally Identifiable Information (PII).
- **Telemetry**: We collect anonymous technical telemetry (e.g., system metrics, model performance, token usage).
- **AI Content**: AI prompts and responses are logged for system optimization and fine-tuning.
- **Sanitization**: All logged AI content is automatically scrubbed of common secrets (AWS keys, GCP keys) and local user home paths (e.g., `/home/[USER]`) before being stored.
- **Retention**: Logs are stored in our secure database and purged according to internal maintenance cycles (typically 30-90 days).

## 2. Age Restriction (COPPA & GDPR-K)
**Rta is strictly for users aged 13 and older.**
- If you are under 13, **STOP**. You are not permitted to use this service.
- We do not knowingly collect data from children.
- If we detect a user under 13, all associated telemetry is wiped immediately.

## 3. Data Usage & Third Parties
- We do not sell data.
- Data is used solely to improve AI accuracy, system stability, and for debugging.
- We use third-party infrastructure (e.g., Vercel, Supabase, AI Model Providers) which may collect technical metadata (IP addresses, headers) as part of their standard operations.

## 4. User Rights
You may request a wipe of your account data and associated telemetry at any time via the support portal. Since telemetry is linked to anonymous session IDs, we cannot "extract" specific logs without your account context.

## 5. Security
We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. However, no system is 100% secure. Use Rta at your own risk.
